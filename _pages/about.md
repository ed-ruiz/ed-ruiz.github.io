 I am a Computer Vision Engineer with over 10 years of experience building perception systems for robotic and autonomous platforms. My work spans real-time ML for semantic segmentation and object detection, stereo depth estimation, Visual-Inertial SLAM, and 3D reconstruction. I am particularly interested in Spatial AI and the integration of vision-language models into 3D perception pipelines for autonomous systems.

 I currently work on 3D Spatial AI for built environment at [DRC](https://drc.co/). Previously, I spent five years at [Beam](https://www.beam.ltd/) (formerly Rovco), where I developed and deployed real-time vision and SLAM systems for underwater robotic inspection.

 I hold a PhD in Computer Vision from [The University of Bristol](https://vilab.blogs.bristol.ac.uk/), where I was advised by [Walterio Mayol-Cuevas](http://people.cs.bris.ac.uk/~wmayol), an MSc in Machine Learning from the University of Bristol, and a BEng in Mechatronics from the National Autonomous University of Mexico.
